The Reception

Enmax Conservatory
Calgary Zoo

Nestled within the Wilder Institute's Calgary Zoo, the Enmax Conservatory is a stunning glass-encased space surrounded by lush tropical gardens. It offers a magical backdrop that brings the wild beauty of nature indoors.

Guests will enjoy the unique atmosphere of the zoo after hours — a one-of-a-kind evening among the animals and exotic plants.

Travel & Accommodation

✈️

Getting Here

Calgary International Airport (YYC) is approximately 20 minutes from the venue by car or rideshare. Direct flights are available from most major Canadian cities.

🏨

Where to Stay

Several hotels are conveniently located near the Calgary Zoo and downtown Calgary. We recommend booking early as May is a popular time to visit.

  • Hotel Arts, Calgary
  • Marriott Downtown
  • Hampton Inn Calgary University NW
🌿

Explore Calgary

Late May is a beautiful time in Calgary. Consider exploring Prince's Island Park, the Bow River pathway, or the vibrant 17th Avenue district during your stay.
